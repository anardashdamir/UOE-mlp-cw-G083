Training Pipeline for NL-Filter: Natural Language to Structured Filter Extraction

Stage 1 - Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT):
- Base model: Qwen3.5 family (0.8B, 2B, 4B parameters), frozen weights
- LoRA adapters applied to all linear layers: rank=16, alpha=32, dropout=0.05
- Trainable parameters: 12.5M (0.8B), 22M (2B), 32.5M (4B) — less than 1.6% of total
- Input: natural language query + dataset schema → Output: structured filter expression
- Training: 1 epoch, batch size 32, learning rate 5e-5, cosine schedule
- Two modes: standard (direct output) and thinking (chain-of-thought in <think> tags)
- Framework: HuggingFace TRL + PEFT on NVIDIA L40S 48GB

Stage 2 - GRPO (Group Relative Policy Optimization):
- Applied only to best SFT model (4B + thinking)
- SFT adapter merged into base model, then new LoRA adapter applied
- For each prompt, generates 8 candidate completions
- Reward function: R = 0.7 × ClauseF1 + 0.3 × ExactMatch bonus
- KL penalty beta=0.01 to prevent divergence from SFT policy
- Early stopping at 50 steps (KL rising, entropy dropping)
- Result: +5.9% exact match improvement over SFT baseline
